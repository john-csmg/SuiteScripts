About Us

Ship & Carry is a Saudi company specialized in providing logistical solutions within the regions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia through a smart and easy-to-use application, through which all shipping operations are carried out, from simple acquisitions to the transportation of heavy equipment.


Our vision

To be the first choice in the shipping and transportation sector in the Arab world and to provide all solutions and facilities,and to keep pace with the objectives of the Kingdom's 2030 vision in developing the logistics sector by meeting the needs of individuals and enterprises using the latest technologies and adhering to the highest standards of accuracy and quality.

Our Message

We strive to meet all transportation and delivery needs of individuals and establishments throughout the Arab world, with care and ease through a single platform by providing our clients with a guaranteed level of service to reach the best offers available in the market and we also enable them to choose the service that best fits their budget.
